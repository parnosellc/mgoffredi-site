The core idea

Spoiler: everyone knows it now. The first second and the first 3 seconds are the most important part of a scalable ad.

What your first 3 seconds need

  • Fear-based hook frameworks (reward framing usually loses).
  • “Stop doing X” style interruption (forces a pause and a rethink).
  • Clear visual suspense (weird food + action + “wait, what?”).

The third one is GOLD. Don’t skip the visuals section.

More building blocks (easy wins)

  • Callout: broad (“if you’re a woman…”) or repelling/attracting for niche audiences (“if you want _”, “if you think _”).
  • Instructions: “Stop scrolling…” buys you 1-2 seconds and gives direction.
  • B-roll engagement: keep visual change happening, even while speaking.
  • CTA: create curiosity, then give just enough detail to prove it’s not clickbait.
  • Double CTA: ask them again to click/message.
